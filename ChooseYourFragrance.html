Choose Your Fragrance Wisely

Selecting Scents: Setting the Perfect Mood



Trying to Set A Particular Mood? Try these scents…

Romantic

Fragrance Notes: Soft florals like Rose, Jasmine, Ylang-Ylang, combined with warm and sweet notes such as Vanilla, Musk, and Sandalwood.

Energetic

Fragrance Notes: Citrusy and invigorating scents like Lemon, Orange, Grapefruit, combined with fresh and herbal notes such as Mint and Eucalyptus.

Calm and Relaxing

Fragrance Notes: Soothing and calming notes like Lavender, Chamomile, Bergamot, combined with creamy and relaxing scents such as Sandalwood and Vanilla.

Confident

Fragrance Notes: Bold and empowering scents like Patchouli, Cedarwood, Leather, and spices such as Black Pepper or Cardamom.

Festive/Celebratory

Fragrance Notes: Warm and spicy aromas like Cinnamon, Nutmeg, Clove, combined with sweet and festive notes such as Vanilla and Sweet Fruits.

Fresh and Clean

Fragrance Notes: Crisp and clean notes like Citrus (e.g., Lemon, Lime), Green Tea, Aquatic Notes, and light florals like Lily of the Valley.

Sensual

Fragrance Notes: Alluring and seductive notes like Musk, Amber, Patchouli, combined with sweet and rich scents like Vanilla.

Playful

Fragrance Notes: Fun and whimsical notes such as Fruity (e.g., Apple, Berry), Floral bouquets, and playful gourmand scents.

Professional

Fragrance Notes: Clean and sophisticated scents like Citrus, Light Florals (e.g., Jasmine), Green Notes, and subtle Woody undertones.

Comforting

Fragrance Notes: Warm and comforting notes like Vanilla, Cinnamon, Tonka Bean, and Benzoin for a cozy and inviting feel.

Adventurous

Fragrance Notes: Exotic and adventurous scents like Spices (e.g., Cardamom), Leather, Woods, and unique, rare florals.

Elegant

Fragrance Notes: Elegant and refined florals such as Rose and Jasmine, complemented by powdery notes like Orris and sophisticated powdery accords.

Nostalgic

Fragrance Notes: Personalized scents that evoke specific memories, perhaps reminiscent of certain places, events, or personal experiences.

Uplifting

Fragrance Notes: Energizing and mood-lifting notes like Citrus, Fruity (e.g., Grapefruit), Mint, and light, uplifting florals.

Mysterious

Fragrance Notes: Dark and mysterious notes such as Oud, Leather, Patchouli, and deep, woody accords.

Focused/Concentrated

Fragrance Notes: Clean and fresh notes like White Musk, Light Florals (e.g., Lily), Herbal elements, and subtle, refreshing accords.

Youthful

Fragrance Notes: Youthful and vibrant notes such as Fruity (e.g., Peach), Citrus, Light Florals, and playful gourmand accords.

Revitalizing

Fragrance Notes: Revitalizing and invigorating notes like Mint, Eucalyptus, Herbal elements, and zesty Citrus.

Cozy

Fragrance Notes: Warm and cozy aromas like Spices (e.g., Clove), Vanilla, Tonka Bean, and Woods for a snug and comforting ambiance.
