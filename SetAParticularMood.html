Trying to Set A Particular Mood? Try these perfumes…




Romantic:  Fragrance Notes: Soft florals like Rose, Jasmine, Ylang-Ylang, combined with warm and sweet notes such as Vanilla, Musk, and Sandalwood.

Energetic:  Fragrance Notes: Citrusy and invigorating scents like Lemon, Orange, Grapefruit, combined with fresh and herbal notes such as Mint and Eucalyptus.

Calm and Relaxing:  Fragrance Notes: Soothing and calming notes like Lavender, Chamomile, Bergamot, combined with creamy and relaxing scents such as Sandalwood and Vanilla.

Confident:  Fragrance Notes: Bold and empowering scents like Patchouli, Cedarwood, Leather, and spices such as Black Pepper or Cardamom.

Festive/Celebratory:  Fragrance Notes: Warm and spicy aromas like Cinnamon, Nutmeg, Clove, combined with sweet and festive notes such as Vanilla and Sweet Fruits.

Fresh and Clean:  Fragrance Notes: Crisp and clean notes like Citrus (e.g., Lemon, Lime), Green Tea, Aquatic Notes, and light florals like Lily of the Valley.

Sensual:  Fragrance Notes: Alluring and seductive notes like Musk, Amber, Patchouli, combined with sweet and rich scents like Vanilla.

Playful:  Fragrance Notes: Fun and whimsical notes such as Fruity (e.g., Apple, Berry), Floral bouquets, and playful gourmand scents.

Professional:  Fragrance Notes: Clean and sophisticated scents like Citrus, Light Florals (e.g., Jasmine), Green Notes, and subtle Woody undertones.
