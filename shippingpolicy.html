American Fragrances Shipping Policy

Last Updated: 02/14/2025

Introduction

Welcome to American Fragrances, your premier destination for luxurious fragrances and beauty products. Our commitment to your satisfaction extends to reliable and transparent shipping services. This comprehensive Shipping Policy provides a detailed overview of our shipping procedures and expectations for your shopping experience.

Shipping Locations

  • Domestic Shipping: We proudly offer domestic shipping services within The United States of America. Orders shipped within our domestic service area typically arrive within 3 to 10 business days from the date of purchase depending on the address.
  • International Shipping: For our international customers, we extend our services worldwide. International shipping times may vary based on the destination and the chosen shipping method. Please allow 15 business days for the delivery of international orders.

Order Processing Time

Orders are processed promptly within 1 to 2 business days from the date of purchase. During peak periods or due to extenuating circumstances, processing times may be slightly extended. You will be notified if this is the case.

Shipping Methods and Costs

  • Standard Shipping: We offer standard shipping options with shipping costs calculated based on the total cost of your order. Standard shipping typically delivers within 3 to 5 business days.
  • Expedited Shipping: For those seeking expedited delivery, we provide expedited shipping options for an additional fee. Specific delivery fees and timeframes for expedited shipping are available at the checkout.

Tracking and Notifications

  • Tracking Information: Upon shipping your order, you will receive a tracking number via email. This tracking number grants you access for real-time updates regarding the status and location of your shipment.
  • Shipping Notifications: To keep you informed, we send email notifications detailing the progress of your order, including confirmation of shipment and final delivery.

Shipping Address

  • Address Accuracy: To ensure a seamless delivery process, it is imperative to provide an accurate and complete shipping address when placing your order. We cannot assume responsibility for orders shipped to incorrect or incomplete Address.
  • Address Changes: In the event you require a change to your shipping address after placing an order, please contact our customer support team promptly. We will make every effort to accommodate your request if the order has not yet been shipped.

Contact Information

Should you have any questions, concerns, or require additional information regarding our shipping procedures, please do not hesitate to reach out to our dedicated customer support team at Contact@americanfragrances.com. Your satisfaction is our priority, and we are committed to ensuring a smooth and reliable shopping experience.

Policy Updates

We reserve the right to update this Shipping Policy as necessary. The "Last Updated" date displayed at the top of this page indicates when modifications were made. By continuing to use our website and services, you acknowledge your acceptance of the prevailing Shipping Policy.

Thank you for choosing American Fragrances. Your trust is deeply valued, and we eagerly anticipate the opportunity to provide you with exceptional products and a seamless shipping experience.
