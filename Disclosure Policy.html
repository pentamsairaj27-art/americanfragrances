American Fragrances Disclosure Policy

Last Updated: 02/14/2025

Introduction

Welcome to American Fragrances, we are deeply committed to maintaining the highest standards of transparency, integrity, and customer trust. This comprehensive disclosure policy aims to provide you with a detailed understanding of how we operate, including our data collection and analytics practices, product recommendations, affiliate relationships, and more.

Data Collection and Analytics

American Fragrances collects and analyzes data derived from user questionnaires and surveys. This valuable information helps us enhance your shopping experience by tailoring product recommendations to your unique preferences. Data collected may encompass fragrance and beauty product preferences, demographics, browsing behaviors, and customer feedback.

We treat your data with the utmost confidentiality and adhere to rigorous data security measures. Please refer to our Privacy Policy for a comprehensive overview of how we collect, utilize, and safeguard your personal information.

Personalized Product Recommendations

Leveraging insights gleaned from user questionnaires and surveys, we employ advanced analytics to provide you with personalized product recommendations. These recommendations are carefully curated to align with your stated preferences and elevate your shopping journey.

It is important to note that while we strive to deliver tailored product suggestions, we encourage you to exercise your discretion and personal taste when making purchasing decisions.

Affiliate Relationships

American Fragrances may engage in affiliate marketing programs. This means that certain links on our website, when clicked and resulting in a purchase, may earn us a commission or referral fee.

It is essential to emphasize that our recommendations and product listings, including those featuring affiliate links, prioritize the provision of high-quality and pertinent product options to our customers.

Advertising and Sponsored Content

Our website may feature advertising and sponsored content, provided by third-party advertisers. These promotional materials are explicitly identified as such and may include links to external websites.

We maintain absolute transparency regarding sponsored content and urge our users to be cognizant of potential commercial interests when engaging with sponsored posts.

Content Accuracy

American Fragrances is committed to delivering content of the highest accuracy and integrity. However, we cannot guarantee the absolute precision, comprehensiveness, or reliability of information sourced from external references or linked websites.

Terms and Conditions

Please take the time to review our comprehensive Terms and Conditions for a detailed elucidation of your responsibilities, rights, and obligations concerning the use of our website, product purchases, and other legal considerations.

Policy Updates
