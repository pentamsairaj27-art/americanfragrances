          What is The AmeriFrag Barcode?

          The AmeriFrag Barcode is a revolutionary, real-time digital fingerprint that captures your unique scent preferences, matching you with the perfect fragrances through advanced user analytics.

          Personal Barcode

          Personality
          Zodiac
          Strength
          Passion
          Entertainment
          Style
          Color
          Smell
          Mood
          Season

          Personal Barcode: When you complete our registration questionnaire, your scent preferences are instantly transformed into a Personal Barcode—a unique digital scent profile tailored just for you. You’ll be matched with other users who share similar preferences, and their favorite products will appear on your profile, giving you immediate access to new fragrances that perfectly align with your tastes.

          Product Barcode

          Style
          Note
          Mood
          Season
          Occasion
          Age Group
          Smell Intensity
          Longevity
          Spray Time
          Presentation

          Each fragrance has its own Product Barcode—a dynamic digital signature that evolves in real-time based on global user feedback. This barcode reflects the most-loved aspects of each fragrance, from its notes to its mood, offering you an interactive, up-to-date interpretation of the scent. As global preferences shift, so does the barcode, ensuring a fresh and relevant way to explore each fragrance’s personality.

          How They Work Together

          Your Personal Barcode seamlessly interacts with the Product Barcodes, matching you with fragrances that are tailored to your digital scent profile. This seamless connection creates an immersive, interactive experiencewhere you can digitally "smell" and discover scents that match your style and preferences, all within a digital world of aromas.

          Disclaimer

          This concept bridges the gap between the physical and the digital shopping experience. Your unique personal barcode will be generated and used to match you with the highest-used and most-liked options based on each of your preferences. Each product’s barcode is comprised of data from users across the globe and constantly changes in real time along with the tastes and preferences of our users, creating the most authentic digital aroma experience.

          Before each user purchases a product, we provide a short survey with 10 questions, one for each AmeriFrag category, that will alter the barcode depending on the most chosen options for each category. The color of each part of the barcode is coded according to the color pallets shown below. The length of each part of the barcode for a product changes based on the most popular option for each analytic category among all of the users who surveyed that particular product.

          It essentially considers both the most popular options overall and the highest percentages of that option amongst the users of each product to create a product-specific division of the barcode, thereby giving it a unique identity within the market.
