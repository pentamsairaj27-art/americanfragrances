What exactly is a perfume?

A perfume is a fragrant liquid substance made from a combination of various aromatic compounds, such as essential oils, aroma chemicals, and solvents, and is created through a complex process that involves blending specific aromatic ingredients in precise proportions to achieve a desired fragrance profile.
