American Fragrances Accessibility Policy

Last Updated: 02/14/2025

Introduction

At American Fragrances, we are committed to providing an inclusive and accessible shopping experience for all our customers. We strive to ensure that our website and services are accessible to everyone, including those with disabilities. Our goal is to create an online environment that is easy to navigate and use, regardless of technology or ability.

Web Accessibility Standards

American Fragrances is dedicated to following the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA, as established by the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C). These guidelines help ensure that our website is accessible to users with a wide range of disabilities, including those who use assistive technologies such as screen readers, magnifiers, and voice recognition software.

Key Accessibility Features

  • Keyboard Navigation: Our website is fully navigable using a keyboard, ensuring that users who cannot use a mouse can still access all content and features.
  • Readable Text: Our site uses clear, simple language and offers the ability to zoom in and resize text to improve readability for users with visual impairments.
  • Responsive Design: Our website is designed to be fully responsive, ensuring that it is accessible on a variety of devices, including mobile phones and tablets.

Ongoing Commitment

We understand that accessibility is an ongoing process, and we are committed to continually improving our website’s accessibility. We regularly review and update our site to ensure that it meets or exceeds current accessibility standards.

Conclusion

At American Fragrances, we believe that everyone should have equal access to the fragrances they love. We are dedicated to ensuring that our website is accessible to all and will continue to work towards greater inclusivity in our digital presence. Thank you for choosing American Fragrances.
